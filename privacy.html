Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

DDCS Cleaning ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you use this website or book our services, why we collect it, and what choices you have. We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and any applicable provincial privacy legislation.

What we collect

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email, and service address when you book a clean.
  • Booking details: type of cleaning, frequency, date, special instructions you choose to share.
  • Communication records: messages you send us via WhatsApp, email, or SMS for service quality and dispute resolution.
  • Site usage: essential cookies only, no cross-site tracking, no advertising IDs.

Why we collect it

  • To deliver the cleaning service you booked (legitimate-business-need basis).
  • To send appointment confirmations and arrival notifications (consent).
  • To handle invoices and tax records (legal obligation under the Income Tax Act).
  • To improve our service quality based on your feedback (consent).

Who sees your data

Your data stays inside DDCS Cleaning. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with marketing partners. The cleaning crew assigned to your booking sees your service address and any access instructions you provide. Our accounting handles invoices.

How long we keep it

  • Active customer records: as long as you remain a customer plus one year.
  • Invoice and tax records: seven years (Canada Revenue Agency requirement).
  • Communications history: three years for service quality review.

Your rights

Under PIPEDA you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or update your information.
  • Withdraw consent for any consent-based processing (subject to legal-obligation limits like tax records).
  • Challenge our compliance and ask for an explanation of how we use your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca.

To exercise any of these rights, message us on WhatsApp at +1 (403) 555-0123 or use the contact details below. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies needed to make the site work (consent-banner status, basic browser session). We do not use advertising cookies, behavioural tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that profile users across sites.

Data location

Your data is stored on Canadian or Canadian-equivalent infrastructure. Hosting is provided by GitHub Pages with a global content-delivery network; the underlying data centres for backup and contact-form submissions are located in North America.

Contact

For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Privacy Officer at +1 (403) 555-0123 on WhatsApp.
